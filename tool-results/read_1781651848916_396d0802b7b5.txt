  1360→**EMI Calculator Formula:**
  1361→
  1362→EMI = [P × R × (1+R)^N] / [(1+R)^N - 1]
  1363→Where P = Principal, R = monthly interest rate, N = number of months.
  1364→Example: ₹50,000 laptop, 18 months, 14% yearly.
  1365→
  1366→R = 14%/12 = 1.166% = 0.01166
  1367→EMI = [50,000 × 0.01166 × (1.01166)^18] / [(1.01166)^18 - 1] = ₹3,174/month
  1368→**Down Payment & Foreclosure:**
  1369→- Down Payment: EMI pe bhi 10-20% down payment dena padta hai. Kam down payment = zyada EMI.
  1370→- Foreclosure Charges: EMI pehle band karna? 2-5% penalty lagti hai. Read fine print.
  1371→Student EMIs — Kitna Safe Hai?
  1372→Recommendation: Student life mein EMI avoid karo. Agar zaroori hai (laptop for coding), toh: (1) Maximum 6-month EMI lo, (2) Total EMI 20% income se kam, (3) No- cost EMI bhi actual cost check karo.

  1417→## 5.6 Credit Score / CIBIL Score — Complete Guide
  1418→
  1419→Credit score ek number hai (300-900) jo batata hai ki tum kitne reliable borrower ho.
  1420→India mein CIBIL sabse popular credit bureau hai. Experian, Equifax, CRIF High Mark bhi hain.
  1421→**CIBIL Score Ranges:**
  1422→Range Rating Meaning Loan Approval
  1423→300-550 Very Poor Defaults, limited history Very difficult
  1424→551-620 Poor High risk, missed payments Difficult, high interest
  1425→621-649 Fair Moderate risk, some issues Possible, higher rates
  1426→650-749 Good Responsible borrower Easy, standard rates
  1427→750-900 Excellent Best borrower Easy, best rates, high limit
  1428→**Kyun Matter Karta Hai:**
  1429→- Loan approval — 750+ = 90% approval chance
  1430→- Interest rate — 750+ pe 8% interest, 600 pe 14% interest
  1431→- Credit card limit — score zyada = limit zyada
  1432→- Rental approval — landlords CIBIL check karte hain
  1433→- Job background check — kuch companies (especially finance) CIBIL dekhte hain
  1434→Kaise Build Karein as Student — 7 Steps:
  1435→1. Student credit card against FD — ₹10,000-₹20,000 FD. Secure card, low risk, CIBIL build hota hai.
  1436→2. Authorized user on parent's card — Parent ka card pe authorized user bano. Unki history tumhari history banegi.
  1437→
  1438→3. Pay bills on time EVERY TIME — Phone, WiFi, electricity — sab on time.
  1439→Late payment = CIBIL hit.
  1440→4. Credit utilization below 30% — ₹50,000 limit = ₹15,000 max use. 10% utilization = excellent.
  1441→5. Start small — ₹5,000 limit se shuru karo. Bade limit ka temptation nahi hoga.
  1442→
  1443→6. Avoid multiple applications — Ek saath 5 credit cards apply karo =
  1444→"credit hungry" tag. 6 months gap rakho.
  1445→7. Check score annually — Free on Paisabazaar, BankBazaar, CIBIL website (1 free report/year).
  1446→7 Tips to Improve Score:
  1447→1. Always pay full credit card bill.
  1448→2. Old credit accounts close mat karo — history length matters.
  1449→3. Mix of credit types (card + loan) achha hota hai.
  1450→4. Settle disputes immediately — even ₹100 ka dispute CIBIL pe rehta hai.
  1451→5. Don't be guarantor for others — unka default = tumhara CIBIL kharab.
  1452→6. Limit credit inquiries — har inquiry 5-10 point drop.
  1453→7. Consistent income proof — ITR, salary slip = stable borrower image.
  1454→5 Common CIBIL Myths Busted:
  1455→1. "CIBIL check karna score kharab karta hai" — Soft inquiry (self check) se koi effect nahi. Hard inquiry (bank check) se thoda effect.
  1456→2. "No loan = good CIBIL" — Nahi! No credit history = no CIBIL score. "No score" bhi problem hai.
  1457→3. "Score ek baar badha toh fixed hai" — Nahi! Har month update hota hai.
  1458→Default = instant drop.
  1459→4. "CIBIL sirf loan ke liye hai" — Nahi! Rental, job, insurance premium sab pe asar padta hai.
  1460→5. "CIBIL sudharne mein 5 saal lagte hain" — Nahi! 6-12 months mein significant improvement possible hai.

  1505→1. Avalanche Method — Mathematically Best:
  1506→
  1507→Highest interest rate wale debt ko pehle bharo. Total interest sabse kam lagti hai.

  1509→Strategy: Minimum sab mein pay karo, extra ₹2,000 credit card pe lagao. Credit card
  1510→clear hone ke baad personal loan pe ₹2,000 extra. Total debt ₹50,000 clear in 24 months vs 48 months (minimum payment method).
  1511→
  1512→
  1513→2. Snowball Method — Psychologically Best:
  1514→Smallest amount wale debt ko pehle bharo. Quick wins motivation dete hain.
  1515→Example: ₹5,000 credit card 1 > ₹8,000 credit card 2 > ₹20,000 personal loan > ₹50,000 education loan.
  1516→Strategy: ₹5,000 wala pehle clear karo (1 month). Celebration! Phir ₹8,000 wala (2 months). Momentum build hota hai.
  1517→
  1518→3. Balance Transfer:
  1519→Lower interest card pe shift karo. 0% balance transfer cards available hain (6 months). ₹20,000 36% se 0% pe shift = ₹3,600 interest save in 6 months.
