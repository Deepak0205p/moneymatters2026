  2713→

  2857→## 10.3 Form 16 Kya Hai — Kaise Padhein
  2858→
  2859→Form 16 woh document hai jo aapka employer deta hai jo batata hai ki saal bhar mein aapki kitni salary thi, kitna TDS cut hua, aur kitna tax aapne pay kiya. Yeh ITR file karne ke liye zaroori hai.
  2860→Form 16 mein 2 parts hote hain:
  2861→- Part A: Employer ki details aur TDS info. TAN number, PAN, total TDS deposited.
  2862→- Part B: Salary breakdown, deductions, aur tax calculation. Basic, HRA, LTA, deductions (80C, 80D), taxable income, tax payable.
  2863→**Kaise Padhein:**
  2864→1. Gross salary check karo — yeh aapki total income.
  2865→2. Exemptions dekho — HRA, LTA, standard deduction (₹50,000).
  2866→3. Deductions verify karo — 80C, 80D, 80E jo aapne claim kiye.
  2867→4. Taxable income = Gross - Exemptions - Deductions.
  2868→5. Tax calculated = final tax liability.
  2869→6. TDS = tax already paid. Agar TDS > tax liability = refund. Agar TDS < tax = tax payable.
  2870→Job lagne ke baad April-June mein employer se Form 16 mango — yeh aapka right hai. Form 16 padhna seekho — usme likha hai ki sarkar ne aapka paisa kahan lagaya aur kitne deductions claim kiye.

  2872→## 10.4 ITR Filing — Kyun Aur Kaise
  2873→
  2874→ITR (Income Tax Return) woh form hai jo aap sarkar ko apni annual income aur tax ki detail dene ke liye bharte hain. Agar aapki income taxable limit se kam hai toh bhi
  2875→ITR file karna beneficial hai.
  2876→4 Reasons to File ITR Even with Zero Tax:
  2877→1. Tax refund claim karne ke liye — agar TDS cut hua hai aur income taxable nahi, toh refund milega. ₹2,000 TDS = ₹2,000 refund.
  2878→2. Loan approval ke liye — bank ITR dekhti hai. Home loan, car loan, personal loan — ITR = income proof.
  2879→3. Visa ke liye — foreign travel mein ITR proof chahiye. US, UK, Schengen — sab ITR maangte hain.
  2880→4. Financial record ke liye — official income history. Future mein business start karna ho toh ITR useful.
  2881→ITR Filing Process — 7 Steps:
  2882→Step 1: Go to incometax.gov.in (official website).
  2883→Step 2: Register with PAN (already registered hoga agar pehle kiya hai).
  2884→Step 3: Select ITR-1 (for salary income) ya ITR-2 (for capital gains).
  2885→Step 4: Fill income details from Form 16.
  2886→Step 5: Claim deductions under 80C, 80D, 80E etc.
  2887→Step 6: Verify tax calculation — payable ya refund.
  2888→Step 7: Verify with Aadhaar OTP ya digital signature. Submit!
  2889→Free hai — kisi CA ki zaroorat nahi simple salary walo ko. Time: 15-30 minutes.
  2890→Deadline: July 31 of every year (for previous financial year). Late filing = ₹1,000- ₹5,000 penalty.
  2891→ITR-1 vs ITR-2 vs ITR-3:
  2892→- ITR-1 (Sahaj): Salary + one house property + other sources (interest). Most students/freshers.
  2893→- ITR-2: Capital gains (SIP, stocks), multiple house property. Agar equity sell kiya ho.
  2894→- ITR-3: Business/profession income. Freelancers, business owners.
  2895→- ITR-4 (Sugam): Presumptive income. Freelancers with 44ADA.
  2896→**Common ITR Mistakes:**
  2897→1. Wrong ITR form select karna.
  2898→2. TDS mismatch — Form 16 vs Form 26AS.
  2899→3. Deductions overclaim karna — fake 80C receipts.
  2900→4. Bank interest declare nahi karna — AI se pakde jaoge.
  2901→5. Verify nahi karna — ITR file hua hi nahi!
